Disaster management involves planning, organizing, and coordinating efforts to prepare for, respond to, and recover from disasters. Here are some key aspects:

Risk Assessment: Identifying potential hazards, their likelihood, and potential impact on people, infrastructure, and the environment.

Preparedness: Developing plans, training personnel, conducting drills, and stockpiling resources (like food, water, and medical supplies) to be ready for emergencies.
